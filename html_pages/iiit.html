IIIT, bangalore May 2010-July 2010

Distribute Systems/Fault Tolerance Algebra

  • I got an opportunity to pursue a 2 months research internship in second year of college with Prof. Shrisha Rao, at International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore.
  • I was assigned the task of developing algebra for degradation analysis of fault tolerant computer systems, which I carried out successfully.
  • The apogee of my internship was when I proposed to consider the use of probabilistic choice in selection of distributed systems in parallel, instead of considering the generic equiprobable systems.
  • Intrigued with the idea, Prof. Shrisha Rao motivated me to develop a comprehensive algebra to handle such distributed systems.
  • A major outcome of this project was publication of a manuscript titled ‘Degradation analysis of probabilistic choice systems’ in the renowned journal, International Journal of Reliability, Quality and Safety Engineering. (http://dx.doi.org/10.1142/s0218539314500120)
